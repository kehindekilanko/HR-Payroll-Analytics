# 📊 HR Payroll Analytics Dashboard  

---

## 👋 Introduction
Payroll data is more than just numbers—it reflects how an organization allocates resources, compensates employees, and manages growth.

In this project, I developed an **interactive HR Payroll Dashboard using Power BI** to transform raw payroll data into meaningful insights that support better business and HR decision-making.

---

## 🎯 The Problem
Organizations often lack clear visibility into:
- How payroll is distributed across departments  
- Compensation trends across employment types  
- Key drivers of salary costs  
- Preferred employee payment methods  

This results in **limited insight for strategic planning and cost optimization**.

---

## 💡 My Approach
I approached this project as a real-world business case:

### 🔹 Data Preparation
- Cleaned and structured payroll data  
- Ensured consistency across salary components  

### 🔹 Data Modeling
- Built relationships between employee data and payroll metrics  
- Designed a scalable data model  

### 🔹 KPI Development
Defined key metrics:
- Total Basic Salary  
- Total Allowances  
- Total Deductions  
- Net Pay  

### 🔹 Dashboard Design
- Focused on simplicity and clarity  
- Designed for business users (HR & Finance stakeholders)  
- Enabled interactive filtering and exploration  

---

## 📊 The Solution
An **interactive Power BI dashboard** that enables users to:

- Monitor overall payroll performance  
- Analyze salary distribution across departments  
- Compare employment types (Full-Time, Part-Time, Internship)  
- Understand payment method preferences  
- Identify top-earning employees  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Bank Transfer is the most preferred payment method  
- Full-Time employees contribute the largest share of payroll  
- Marketing and HR departments have the highest salary distribution  
- Allowances and deductions show noticeable growth trends  
- A small group of employees accounts for a significant portion of total pay  

---

## 📈 Business Impact
This dashboard helps organizations:
- Make **data-driven compensation decisions**  
- Improve **budget allocation across departments**  
- Increase **transparency in payroll management**  
- Identify **cost drivers and inefficiencies quickly**
